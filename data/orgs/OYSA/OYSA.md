# OYSA (Leagues, Cups, Academy): Referee Information

**RefTown:** OYSA leagues/cups/academy via RefTown (Assoc 3695). Games also assigned through NWSC and its payor leagues.

**How to get games:** Join OYSA Leagues / NWSC in RefTown. Includes Fall/Spring Leagues and State Cups; pays every two weeks; requires online game reports for pay.

## Game reports and procedures

- **Where to file:** RefTown/NWSC homepage – Missing Game Reports notice. Link to game reports on OYSA Home Page.
- **Deadline:** Saturday games by 9am Monday; Sunday games by 9am Tuesday. File within 24 hours.
- **Misconduct:** Center refs submit OYSA Misconduct Report for each card; call area assignor for send-offs before writing report.
- **Notes:** Indicate crew changes on game report for correct pay. Late reports: CR $10/game, AR $5/game.

---

## Pay and cancellation policy

- **Pay schedule:** Every two weeks for prior two weeks; pay dates every other Friday.
- **Cancellation / pay:** See NWSC procedures (e.g. cancel before 5pm day before = no pay; 5pm–6am = half fee; after 6am = full fee).
