# NorthWest Soccer Central (NWSC): Referee Information

**RefTown:** Top-level organization (umbrella)

**How to get games:** Join NWSC in RefTown. One registration covers all member Payor Leagues (CUSC, Eastside Timbers, LOSC, NCSC, OCSC, OYSA leagues, PT, TVYSL, USYS, WUFC). Request to join specific payor leagues from your profile. Oregon & SW Washington. General playing dates: September–May.

## Game day and emergency contacts

- **Referee hotline:** OYSA Referee Hotline (503) 332-5432 – no-shows, referee abuse (call your area assignor first).
- **Game day hotline:** OYSA Game Day Hotline (971) 732-9897 – questions about games and rules, team has no player cards/roster, send-offs, injury requiring ambulance, abnormal situations.

---

## Game reports and procedures

- **Where to file:** RefTown/NWSC homepage – click **Notice, Missing Game Reports** to see games missing reports. All referees (CRs & ARs) must submit online game reports in RefTown/NWSC for all OYSA League, Cup, and Academy games.
- **Deadline:** Saturday games – by 9am Monday; Sunday games – by 9am Tuesday.
- **Misconduct:** Center refs MUST file Misconduct Report for all cards; MUST call send-offs to Game Day Hotline immediately after the game.
- **Notes:** Indicate any crew changes on your game report so everyone is paid correctly. CRs with missing reports: $10/game; ARs: $5/game.

---

## Pay and cancellation policy

- **Pay schedule:** OYSA pays referee game fees every two weeks for the prior two weeks; pay dates every other Friday.
- **Cancellation / pay:** Effective Jan 11, 2024 – Saturday games: cancel before 5pm Friday = no pay; 5:01pm Friday–6am Saturday = half fee; after 6am Saturday = full fee. Sunday games: before 5pm Saturday = no pay; 5:01pm Saturday–6am Sunday = half fee; after 6am Sunday = full fee.
