**When are your Game Reports Due?**

- **ALL** **REFEREES,** **CRs &** **ARs**, must submit online game reports in RefTown/NWSC for all OYSA League, Cup, and Academy games.
- On the RefTown/NWSC homepage you will see a **Notice, Missing Game Reports.** Click on this to go to your games that are missing reports.
- Game played on**Saturday -**must be filed no later than 9am the following Monday.
- For all **Sunday games** - filed no later than 9am the following Tuesday.
- **ALL****REFS** - please indicate any changes made in the assigned referee crew in your game report so that everyone is paid correctly!
- **Center Refs** MUST file a Misconduct Report for all cards issued during the game.
- **Center Refs**MUST call in any send-offs to the Game Day Hotline immediately after the game.
- Center Refs with missing reports will be charged **$10/game.**
- ARs with missing reports will be charged **$5/game.**

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

**¿Cuándo vencen los informes de juego?**

- **TODOS LOS ÁRBITROS, centrales y asistentes,** deben enviar reportes de partido en línea en RefTown/NWSC para todos los juegos de OYSA League, Cup y Academy.
- En la página principal de RefTown/NWSC verás un aviso de **Reportes de Partidos Faltantes**. Haz clic allí para ir a los partidos que aún no tienen reporte
- Juego jugado en**Sábado -**debe presentarse a más tardar a las 9 a.m. del lunes siguiente
- Para todos juegos**del domingo** - presentado a más tardar a las 9 a. m. del martes siguiente
- **TODAS LAS REFERENCIAS** - por favor indica cualquier cambio realizado en el equipo de árbitros asignado en tu informe de juego para que todos reciban el pago correcto.
- **Árbitros del centro** DEBE presentar un informe de mala conducta para todas las tarjetas emitido durante el juego.
- **Árbitros del centro**DEBEN comunicar cualquier expulsión a la línea directa del día del partido inmediatamente después del partido.
- A los árbitros centrales se les cobrará **$10 por juego.**
- A los AR se les cobrará **$5 por juego.**

**Thank you for working OYSA games this season, we appreciate you!**

- OYSA pays referee game fees every two weeks for the prior two weeks of games from the start of each season. Pay dates are every other Friday.
- A friendly reminder that **GAME REPORTS** should be filed within 24 hours by ALL referees who worked the game.
- The **link to GAME REPORTS** for **YOU TO FILE** is found on your OYSA Home Page.

- **Center Refs** - be sure to report ALL cards issued in the game so our counts are accurate.  Please submit an OYSA Misconduct Report for each card given. This is very important!

- **ALL****REFS** - please indicate any changes that were made to the referee crew on your game report so that everyone is paid correctly!

- Call your area assignor if you had a send-off in your game before writing your game report. Your area assignor will assist you to write a coherent report. Call your area assignor with any other questions.
