## List of NWSC Leagues

**NorthWest Soccer Central (NWSC)** is a consolidated "umbrella" organization. Users registered with NWSC can access several sub-groups (referred to as **Payor Leagues**) which are *not* always listed as separate top-level Reftown organizations in the directory. These include:
*   Clackamas United Soccer Club (CUSC)
*   Eastside Timbers
*   Lake Oswego Soccer Club (LOSC)
*   North Clackamas Soccer Club (NCSC)
*   Oregon City Soccer Club (OCSC)
*   Oregon Youth Soccer Association (OYSA)
*   Willamette United Football Club (WUFC)

To access these, a referee must register with **NWSC** and then "Request to Join" the specific Payor League from their profile.
